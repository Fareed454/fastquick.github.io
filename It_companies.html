TATA Consultancy Services (TCS)

TATA Consultancy Services (TCs) is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company, headquartered in Mumbai, Mahahrashtra, India and largest campus and workforce in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. As of May 2021, TCS is the largest company in the IT sector in the world by market capitalization of $169.2 billion. It is a subsidiary of the Tata Group and operates in 46 countries.

TCS is the second largest Indian company by market Capitialization. Tata Consultancy Services is now placed aong the most valuable IT Services brands worldwide. In 2015, TCS was ranked 64th overall in the Forbes World's Innovative Companies ranking, making it both the highest-ranked IT services company and the top Indian company.

Infosys Services

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology company that provides business consulting, information and outsourcing services. The company is headquartered in Bangalore. Infosys is the second-largest Indian IT company after Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) by 2020 revenue figures and the 602nd largest public company in the world according to Forbes Global 2000 ranking. On 31 December 2020, it's market capitalization was $71.92 billion. The credit rating of the company is A.

Training Centre in Mysuru

As the world's largest corporate university, the Infosys global education centre in the 337 acre campus has 400 instructors and 200+ classrooms, with international benchmarks at its core. Established in 2002, it had trained around 1,25,000 engineering graduates by June 2015, It can train 14,000 employees at a given point of time on various technologies.

Wipro headquarteres on Sarjapur Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka, India

Wipro Limited

Wipro Limitedis an Indian multinational corporation that provides information technology, consulting and business process services. It is headquartered in Bangalore, karnataka, India. In 2013, Wipro separated its non-IT businesses and formed the privately owned Wipro Enterprises. Wipro has shifted to Work from Anywhere model since March 2020 so employees can work from anywhere in the world except Wipro office premises due to COVID pandemic.

Employees

Wipro is the ninth largest employer in India with nearly 195,000 employees.

On 66 July 2020, Thierry Delaporte took over from Abidali Neemuchwala as the new CEO of Wipro. Abidali Neemuchwala was appointed as Wipro's CEO After T.K Kurien stepped down in early 2016. Neemuchwala, who had been group president and CEO from April 2015, was appointed CEO with effect from 1 February 2016.

HCL technologies Campus in Noida

HCL technologies Limited

HCL technologies is an Indian multinational information technology (IT) services and consulting company, headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is a subsidiary of HCL Enterprise. Originally a research and development division of HCL, it emerged as an independent company in 1991 when HCL entered into the software services business. The company has office in 32 countries including United Kingdom, United States, France, and Germany with a worldwide network o R&D, "innovation labs" and "delivery centers", over 159,000 employees and its customers include 250 of the Fortune 500 and 650 of Global 2,000 companies.

Mphasis Limited

Mphasis Limited is an IT serivces company based in Bangalore, India. The company provides infrastructure technology and applications outsourcing services, as well as architecture guidance, application development and integration, and application management services. It serves financial services, telecom, logistics, and tehnology industries. Mphasis was ranked #7 in India IT companies and overall #189 by Fortune India 500 in 2019. In April 2016, Hawlett Packard Enterprise sold the majority of its stake in Mphasis to Blackstone Group LP for around US$1 Billion.

Mphasis provides information technology services to its customers around the world. Mphasis provides integrated solutions that include business process outsourcing, infrastructure technology, and application services. The application services offered by the company includes applications development as well as applications maintanance and support services. The markets served by the company are financial services and insurance, healthcare, manufacturing, government, trasnsportation, communications, and consumer and retailer industries.

Mindtree Limited

Mindtree Ltd is an Indian multinational information technology and outsourcing company, headquartered inn Bangalore, India. It is a part of the Larsen & Turbo Group. Founded in 1999, the company employs approximately 21, 1991 employees with an annual revenue of Rs7839.9 crore (US$1.1 billion).

The company deals in e-commerce, mobile, cloud computing, digital transformation, data analytics, Testing, enterprise application integration and enteprise resource planning, with more than 307 active clients and 43 offices in over 18 countries, as of 31 March 2019.

Services

Mindtree works Application Developement and Maintenance, Data Analytics, Digital Services, Enterprise Apllication Integration and Business Process Management, Engineering R&D, Enterprise Application Services, Testing, and infrastructure Management Services.

Employees

Mindtree has a total of 21,991 employees as of March 2020, of which 32% were women. Its workforce consists of employees from over 80 nationalities working from various offices around the globe. Out of its total workforce, 95% are software professionals and remaining 5% work in support and sales.

Tech Mahindra Limited

Tech Mahindra is an Indian multinational company that provides information technology (IT) and business process outsourcing (BPO) services. A subsidiary of the Mahindra Group, the company is headquartered in Pune and has its registered office in Mumbai.

As of April 2020, Tech Mahindra is a US5.2 billion company with 125,236 employees across 90 countries. The company was ranked #5 in India's IT firms and overall #47 in Fortune India 500 list for 2019. On 25 June 2013, Tech Mahindra announced the completion of a merger with Mahindra Satyam. Tech Mahindra has 973 active clients as of April 2020.
